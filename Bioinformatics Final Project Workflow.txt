Literature on the topic highlights the phosphorylation of HSP27 by MAPK pathway which regulates estrogen signaling and promotes cancer cell survival.Overall workflow:1) Download data from GEO and perform FASTQC to assess quality.2) Assemble the transcriptome using SPAdes.3) Build NCBI BLAST+ database using assembled transcriptome and query HSP27.4) KEGG Pathway analysis.
